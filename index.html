How it works

Three taps between you and your people.

No dispatchers, no strangers. Just the friends and family you choose, looking out for each other.

1

Build your circle

Add the people you trust — close friends, family, your roommate. Make a general circle, or a vacation circle that lasts the length of a trip.

2

Start a Cuídame session

Heading out on a date, a night out, or a walk home? Start a Cuídame — Spanish for "watch over me." Your circle sees a live countdown and check-ins until you mark yourself safe.

3

Mark yourself safe

Made it? Tap "I'm home safe" and the session ends. If something feels off, one tap sends an SOS with your location to your circle.

What's inside

Built for the moments you want someone there.

Circles

Trusted groups of friends and family. Keep an everyday circle, or spin up a vacation circle for a trip.

Cuídame sessions

"Watch over me" — a check-in session with a live countdown and auto-expiry, so your circle knows the moment you're safe.

SOS alert

One tap alerts your circle with your location. Includes a duress-PIN cancel, contact acknowledgment, and a resolution flow.

Safe Zones

Mark trusted places — home, a friend's, work — on a map for each circle, so arrivals are easy to see.

In-circle chat

A private chat per circle and live location sharing, so coordinating a night out stays in one place.

Bilingual, by design

Salí speaks English and Spanish from day one — built with Dominican roots and family in mind.

For the whole family

Salimos (we go out) — head out together, arrive together.

Salimos turns a circle into a supervised group outing. A parent or organizer keeps the whole group on one screen — kids, cousins, abuela (grandma) — so everyone heads out together and everyone's accounted for.

Perfect for a family beach day, a school trip, or a night out with friends where someone's keeping count.

Trust & privacy

We don't sell your data. We never will.

Salí only works if you trust it — so we built it on a promise most apps can't make: we don't sell your information, run ad trackers, or hand it to data brokers. Your circle is the only audience.

We never sell your data

No ad trackers, no data brokers, no selling your location to anyone. We don't, and we never will.

Shared only with your circle

Your live location and check-ins are visible only to the contacts you add — and to no one else.

You're in control

You decide who's in a circle, when a session is live, and when it ends. Stop sharing any time.

Kept only as long as needed

Session data is retained briefly to make the feature work, then removed. Our Privacy Policy spells out exactly how long.

Salí is not an emergency service. It helps the people you trust look out for you — it does not contact police, fire, or medical responders. In a life-threatening emergency, always call your local emergency number first.
